OVERVIEW

Customer Support Triage Environment

An OpenEnv-compatible environment where an AI agent receives real customer support tickets and must classify, prioritize, and draft replies. Built for the Meta × Hugging Face OpenEnv Hackathon.

FIELDTYPEDESCRIPTION

task_namestrclassify / prioritize / resolve
step_numberintCurrent step in episode
ACTION SPACE
FIELDTYPEVALUES

replystrCustomer-facing response (resolve only)
TASKS
EASY
Classify
Identify the correct support category from billing, technical, or general.
Score: exact match → 1.0 or 0.0
MEDIUM
Prioritize
Classify the ticket AND assign the correct urgency level.
Score: 0.5×category + 0.5×priority
HARD
Resolve
Full triage: classify, prioritize, and draft a helpful customer reply.
Score: 0.3×cat + 0.3×pri + 0.4×reply
